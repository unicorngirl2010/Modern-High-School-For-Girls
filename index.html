State Sprint Rowing Championship
Congratulations to our four promising oarswomen.

Sreshtha Sureka, class 10 C, Ishika Das, class 9C, Varija Dhandhania, class 10 B, and Yashita Choraria, class 9 B, won Gold medals in their respective categories in the recently concluded State Sprint Rowing Competition 2021.
