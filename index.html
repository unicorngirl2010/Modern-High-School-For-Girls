Campus Life

Auditorium

Used for weekly assemblies and play practices.

The auditorium is huge and has a big stage.It is used for weekly assemblies and play practices.Small Programs are held here and P.E. audition,Children's day canteen and teacher's meet and greet are also held here. Sometimes the karate also happen here. Children eat their lunch here during Summer.

Canteen

Used to buy lunch.

The canteen has 2 counters and 5 tables. There are donuts. cheeseballs, popcorn, pasta, pizza, cookies, mudpie, pani-puri, noodles, chocolates, brownie etc. There is also an ice cream man near the canteen. He sells every kind of Kwality Walls Ice-cream. Students buy food here and Karate classes are held here sometimes. Durning sports day the teachers coordinate with the students here.

Library

Used in the Library period.

The library has many books, a big projector and a walls of art. It has many tables and five monitresses. The monitresses talk more than the students. Every book has a library card which has to be submitted to the librarian.
